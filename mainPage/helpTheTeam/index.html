How You Can Help

Your support can make a big difference in shaping the future of aspiring business leaders. Here are ways you can contribute:

Donate

Your generous donations help fund student participation in DECA events, travel expenses, and materials. Every little bit counts!

Become a Mentor

Share your expertise and knowledge with DECA students by becoming a mentor. Your guidance can help mold the future leaders of tomorrow!

Join as a Member

Become a part of our vibrant community of like-minded individuals passionate about business and leadership. DECA membership is the first step toward a brighter future.

Become a Judge

As a judge, you play an integral role in evaluating student projects and providing constructive feedback. Help students improve and excel by sharing your insights!
